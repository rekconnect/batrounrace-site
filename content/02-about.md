# Who We Are
Created by people who love Batroun and its community, Batroun Race is led by athletes, engineers, and marketing specialists committed to building a meaningful, high-quality running event driven by care and passion rooted in Batroun, open to all of Lebanon, and growing stronger every year.

Stats: **500+ Runners (2025)** · **1.5M+ Exposure**

**Mission & Vision**
- Mission: build a healthier, more connected community through running and public celebration.
- Vision: make Batroun a reference for coastal sport tourism and positive youth energy.
- Promise: safe routes, great organization, unforgettable vibes.

## The Batroun Race Community
The Batroun Race Community is built by professional athletes, engineers, and creatives who believe in sport as a way to connect people and give movement meaning.

**More Than a Race** — A shared experience that brings together runners, families, volunteers, and local supporters—rooted in Batroun and open to all of Lebanon.
- What you feel on race day: streets filled with runners instead of cars; music and cheering along the route; families, food, and social moments after the race.

**Our Purpose** — We are not chasing titles or records. We are building something we believe in — learning, improving, and growing stronger with every edition.
- What we build each year: safe and enjoyable coastal routes; simple participation for all levels; a festival-like finish zone; community-driven impact through collaborations.

## Who Is Part of the Community?
- **Runners** — Beginners, casual joggers, and athletes; teenagers, adults, and seniors; locals from Batroun and participants from across Lebanon. (Run for: fitness, fun, personal challenge, community spirit.) [img: finish_line.jpg]
- **Volunteers** — Local youth, sports clubs, and community members help with registration & bib distribution, water stations, route safety, crowd support. [img: podium.jpg]
- **Local Businesses & Sponsors** — Cafés, restaurants, gyms, sports brands, beach resorts, and touristic spots collaborate — turning race day into a town-wide celebration. (Benefits: foot traffic, high-trust visibility, content and media coverage.)

## Community Values
- **Health & Wellness** — Encourages an active lifestyle; running as an accessible sport for everyone.
- **Love for Batroun** — Highlights the seaside, old souks, and coastal roads — promoting Batroun as a sports and tourism destination.
- **Unity & Positivity** — Brings people together beyond differences — runners cheer for strangers, families wait at the finish line, and the town celebrates together.
- **Safety & Responsibility** — Safe routes, organized support points, respectful experience. (Commitments: clear route guidance and support points; volunteer coordination and crowd flow; clean streets and responsible celebration.)

## Race Day = Community Festival
- **Atmosphere** — Streets filled with runners, music along the route, families and social moments after the race. After the finish: many stay to swim, eat, and explore Batroun; a full-day celebration by the sea. [img: hang_loose.jpg]
- **The Experience Flow** — Start energy → coastal push → cheering points → finish pride → music and community moments. What makes it special: Batroun scenery + strong vibes; support from volunteers and locals; a finish that feels like a celebration. [img: race_day.jpg]

## Why the Community Matters
- Builds positive youth role models
- Encourages tourism through sports
- Creates safe, healthy public events
- Strengthens Batroun's image as a lively, active city

Long-term vision: grow participation while keeping quality and safety; support more community collaborations and local initiatives; make Batroun a must-visit sport weekend destination.

## Business Model — 3-Phase Sponsorship Funnel
- **Phase 1 (Pre-event):** website exposure, social announcements, flyer distribution, partner mentions.
- **Phase 2 (Race day):** start/finish branding, route exposure, hydration/checkpoints, photo wall moments.

## KPI Framework — What is measured
- **Exposure:** placements delivered, audience size, frequency.
- **Engagement:** content views, engagement rate, on-site interactions.
- **Attribution:** QR scans, UTM clicks, CTR.
- **Conversion:** leads/redemptions/signups + ROI report (≤ 7 days).
